Portfolio

Our portfolio is comprised of a broad collection of ventures.

Legatum Capital

Legatum Capital invests in well-run businesses across a range of sectors, primarily focusing on consumer, technology and financial services.

View

Legatum Institute

The Legatum Institute exists to promote the prosperity of individuals, families, communities and nations.

View

The END Fund

The END Fund’s purpose is to eliminate the five most common neglected tropical diseases by 2030.

View

The Freedom Fund

The Freedom Fund is a leader in the global movement to end modern slavery.

View

The Luminos Fund

The Luminos Fund gives children excluded from school due to poverty, discrimination or conflict, a second chance for a mainstream education.

View

A Family For Every Child

The best place for a child to grow up is in a family. Legatum is funding local organisations in Kenya to learn what works best in shifting from care in "orphanages" to family-based care.

View

Community-led Humanitarian Response

Legatum wants to see a humanitarian response system that supports crisis-affected people’s own initiatives to help their communities recover from a crisis.

View

Advancing Justice

Advancing dignity, justice, and transformational change in criminal justice systems in the global south.
